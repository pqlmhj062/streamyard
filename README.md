**What StreamYard Does Better Than Alternatives**

StreamYard sits in an interesting competitive space. StreamYard's eight-destination limit on the Advanced plan covers the major platforms most creators actually use.

The real differentiation comes in the user experience. StreamYard's interface is notably more intuitive, especially for people joining as guests. If you want to get someone who's never livestreamed before on your show without spending fifteen minutes explaining the technical process, StreamYard makes that much easier than most alternatives.

OBS Studio keeps coming up in these comparisons because it's free and genuinely powerful.
